About Our Mission

Founded in 2019 in Delhi, Basti Ki Pathshala is a certified NGO dedicated to providing quality education to underprivileged children and empowering communities through learning.

Est. 2019
Delhi, India
NGO Certified

Our Mission

To provide quality education and learning opportunities to underprivileged children, empowering them with knowledge, skills, and confidence to break the cycle of poverty and build brighter futures.

Our Vision

A world where every child, regardless of their socio-economic background, has access to quality education and the opportunity to reach their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Our Core Values

Compassion

Caring deeply for every child's wellbeing and future

Excellence

Striving for the highest quality in education and service

Community

Building strong, supportive networks for lasting change

Inclusivity

Ensuring equal opportunities for all children

Our Journey

2019

Foundation Established

Basti Ki Pathshala was founded with a vision to provide quality education to underprivileged children in Delhi.

2020

Digital Initiative

Launched our digital literacy program to bridge the technology gap during the pandemic.

2022

Expansion

Expanded our reach to 10+ communities and introduced skill development programs.

2024

Recognition

Received recognition for our impact in education and community development, reaching 500+ children.
